## Puree

### Ingredients

* 1 kg spinach (frozen)
* 1 200 - 250 ml (standard cup) of milk
* 1/2-1 onion
* 3 cloves of garlic
* salt to taste
* water as needed
* 4 spoons of flour for thickennig
* (optional) 1-2 eggs
